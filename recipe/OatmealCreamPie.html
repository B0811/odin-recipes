Oatmeal Cream Pies

Delightful Cream Pies

These melt in your mouth cookiepiethings were taught to me by my great great great great great great great great great grandpappy, enjoy

Ingredients

  • 9 Oats
  • Cream of the Babe
  • 97 almonds with nipples
  • 1 bar of fresh Tungsten
  1. Bathe the oats in the cream
  2. While they're bathing, milk your almonds
  3. Soak the Tungsten in the fresh almond milk
  4. beat the oats with the Tungsten
  5. Enjoy!
